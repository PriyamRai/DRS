Helping Hands for Wildfire Victims

Campaign Description

This campaign aims to provide immediate relief to families affected by the recent wildfire in Mayur Vihar, Delhi. The fire has left many homeless and in dire need of shelter, food, and essential supplies. Your support will make a significant impact in helping these families rebuild their lives.


Campaign Milestones

Milestone 1: Rs5,00,000 - Emergency Shelter: Provide shelter to 50 families.

Milestone 2: Rs10,00,000 - Food and Supplies: Distribute supplies to 200 families.

Milestone 3: Rs15,00,000 - Medical Services: Ensure medical support for the injured.

Milestone 4: Rs20,00,000 - Long-Term Recovery: Assist in community rebuilding
